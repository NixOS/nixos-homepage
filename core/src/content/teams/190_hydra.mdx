The NixOS Hydra team was formed to organize and continue the development of Hydra and transforming it into a future-proof state.

## Goals

- Strengthen governance
- Maintain the software to keep it alive and fit to the requirements by the infrastructure team
- Engage contributors

## Responsibilities

The team is responsible for:

- Owning and maintaining the hydra repository
- Coordinating with contributors
- Organizing future development
