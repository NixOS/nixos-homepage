The Software Provenance Team aims to improve provenance and supply
chain tracking in Nixpkgs by facilitating information exchange between
tool authors, package maintainers and the nixpkgs core team. As such,
it is responsible for:

- A coherent vision around software provenance in Nixpkgs
- Encouraging and guiding the development of the infrastructure needed
  to generate high-quality Software Bill of Material documents (SBOMs)
- Improving package metadata related to provenance, e.g. for the
  purpose of vulnerability matching
