The NixOS Hardware team was formed to improve device support coordination and hardware compatibility across the NixOS ecosystem.

## Goals

- Improve device support coordination
- Strengthen governance
- Expand partnerships with hardware vendors
- Engage contributors

## Responsibilities

The team is responsible for:

- Owning and maintaining the nixos-hardware repository
- Coordinating with hardware partners
- Establishing contribution workflows for device enablement, testing, and documentation
- Improving hardware support across the NixOS ecosystem

## Get Involved

The team welcomes new contributors interested in improving hardware support in NixOS. Join the Matrix channel or check out the GitHub repository to get started.
