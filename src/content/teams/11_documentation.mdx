The Nix documentation team was formed to flatten the infamous learning curve.

## Goals

- Flatten the infamous learning curve
- Ease Nix learning, increase onboarding success, and user retention
- Improve organization of Nix knowledge
- Lead, guide, and support community efforts

## Contact and Information

Information about team procedures can be found in the [Team
handbook](https://github.com/NixOS/nix.dev/tree/master/maintainers).
Discussions are held on Matrix, Discourse, and on GitHub issues and pull
requests.
